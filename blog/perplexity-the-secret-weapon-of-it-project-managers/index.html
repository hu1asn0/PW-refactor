Perplexity: The Secret Weapon of IT Project Managers

András Simon

Welcome to the P4PMS Newsletter!

I want to share a tool that completely transformed my IT project management workflows. Not another tab in your browser, not another app on your phone – but a genuine strategic partner for connecting information and making decisions.

Perplexity AI – research, content generation, and project management tools in one place.


🎯 Why Perplexity for IT Project Managers?

You face these challenges almost daily:

  • Planning an SAP migration? Perplexity pulls together best practices, risks, and average implementation timelines.

  • Need to decide on new technology? We run deep research, synthesize findings, and extract lessons.

  • Need stakeholder documentation? With Perplexity Pages, you have polished, professional documents in minutes.

  • Team communication? The Email Assistant ensures you don’t waste hours writing emails.

The difference isn’t about features – it’s about integration and agility. One dashboard. One thinking partner. One executor.

📋 1. STEP-BY-STEP EXAMPLE: Creating an SAP Project Stakeholder Proposal in 20 Minutes

Scenario: Monday morning, 9:00 AM. You need an SAP implementation proposal for senior management.

Traditional approach: Research (2-3 hours) → Excel spreadsheet (1-2 hours) → Formatting (30 mins) → Stakeholder feedback loop.

Perplexity approach: Complete in 20 minutes.

Step 1: Begin the Research

Open Perplexity Pro.

Type: “Create an SAP S/4HANA implementation proposal for a 500-person Hungarian tech company. Include phases, resource requirements, budget, risks, and ROI estimates. Format for 2-3 pages.”

Perplexity Deep Research (Pro feature) completes this in 3 minutes, analyzing nearly 100 sources.

Step 2: Convert Research into a Document

The results are comprehensive and cited. Now click: “Convert to Page”

Perplexity automatically formats the content: headings, paragraphs, bullet points. It’s already close to your final version.

Step 3: Fine-tune Your Document

Click the Edit button. Simply:

  • Modify text with company-specific data

  • Upload your company logo

  • Arrange according to your needs

Step 4: Export and Share

Click Export:

  • PDF (ready to print)

  • Markdown (for Notion / Google Docs)

  • DOCX (for further editing)

After about 20 minutes, you have a professional, cited proposal.

🍳 2. COOKBOOK: Vendor Invoice Audit Preparation (Supporting SAP Project Phase)

Scenario: After your SAP implementation tasks, your team needs to establish vendor invoice audit procedures. The audit team isn’t familiar with the system’s capabilities.

Goal: Create a “Quickstart Guide” for vendor invoice management in SAP, including common errors and control checkpoints.

The Steps:

Step 1: Research Required Information

In Perplexity Pro, type:

Create a summary of best practices for SAP S/4HANA vendor invoice management, including:
- Data entry validation processes
- Common errors and prevention methods
- Audit control checkpoints
- Tips for managing SLAs and payment terms

Step 2: Use Perplexity AI Comet Assistant

Now automate document organization with Comet Assistant:

  • Click the “AI Assist” button in your exported document

  • Provide a prompt: “Organize this as an audit checklist format for easy printing”

  • Comet automatically structures content into sections

Step 3: Integrate with Notion (or ClickUp, as an alternative)

Since Perplexity’s API supports Notion integration:

  • Export the document to Markdown

  • Open your Notion database (where your project documentation lives)

  • Copy and paste the Perplexity content

Optionally: Set up continuous sync via Perplexity API (available in Pro), so updates automatically sync.

Step 4: Team Education

  • Create a Teams or Slack message from the Perplexity-generated document

  • Send a quick link to the compiled document

  • Your team can even add comments through the Perplexity Space

💡 Practical Tips for IT Project Managers

  1. Organize with Spaces: Create a dedicated Space for every SAP project. Store research notes, stakeholder feedback, documents here.

  2. Deep Research is your best friend: When making critical decisions (e.g., selecting a new tool), don’t just Google – use Deep Research mode. It analyzes 100+ sources in 3 minutes.

  3. Skip Email Processing: For all stakeholder emails you need to reply to, let Email Assistant handle it. Instead of 15 minutes of email management – 2 minutes done.

  4. Use App Connectors: Connect GitHub, Linear, Gmail, Notion to Perplexity. Single prompt: “Summarize all open project tasks” – instant overview.

🚀 Which Plan and Version?

  • Free version: Good for basic research, but limited daily searches.

  • Perplexity Pro ($20/month): Unlimited searches, Deep Research, File Upload, App Connectors. This is ideal for IT PMs.

  • Perplexity Max: Unlimited Labs (useful for team-level work), early access to new products.
