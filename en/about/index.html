About me

I'm a project manager with more than 15 years of professional experience. My clients approach me with tasks originating from IT, automation and manufacturing environments.

I strongly support simplified workflows, sustainable processes and real results. I continuously implement development opportunities to improve project managers' communication.

In recent years I've also been focusing on the practical application of artificial intelligence: how to integrate AI tools into existing workflows in a way that creates real business value.

Service areas

  • Project Management — Full-scope IT and technical project management, agile and traditional methodologies
  • Planning & Execution — Requirements analysis, scheduling, budgeting, team coordination, the full project lifecycle
  • Monitoring & Reporting — KPI tracking, project progress reporting, custom reporting methodology
  • Consulting & AI Integration — Methodology and process analysis, LLMs and automation integrated into workflows
  • Training — Individual and group trainings, educating decision-makers and key stakeholders on new processes or systems

Experience in a nutshell

Throughout my career I've worked at major corporations such as MOL, E.ON and SANYO, as well as in SME environments. I have international experience in Sweden, Australia (Sydney) and Germany. Project sizes ranged from small teams to 70-person execution teams, with budgets exceeding 2 MEUR.
